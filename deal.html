The Deal

Alex Nye

cast

  • Gene/Gina: a high school chemistry teacher
  • Lou: a high school speech and debate teacher
  • Sam: a drug dealer
  • Rick/Rikki: Sam's muscle


A frigid day in a Denny's parking lot. Gene and Lou stand together waiting.

Lou
We're teachers, Gene! Not drug dealers!
I'm a speech and debate teacher! I should've known better than to listen to you.

Gene
Do you want your students sharing pencils, Lou!?
Nobody buys shit at the bake sale anymore! We're lucky I'm a chemistry teacher and that Tessa in 3rd period also knew how to make meth!

Lou?
Alright, let's just get this over with and get back to D&D tonight.



Sam and Rick walk onto stage and stand facing Gene and Lou from 10ft away.

Sam
So you're the 2 nerds. Let's see the product.

Gene
Oh jeeze. This is some complicated game theory shit.
Maybe we could exchange some gifts first as a trust exercise.

Rick
Ey!? Who the fuck is this guy? You got the goods or no!?

Sam
Alright, look. I know we don't trust each other. But this ain't no barbie tea party.

Gene
Uhh. Okay okay. How about this. We each slide our parts of the exchange across ice at the same time.
That way, we can each make sure that the other has sent their part before we get ours.

Gene lines up a shove across the ice

Lou
Woah, wait. Someone still has to send it out first. What if we push the drugs across the ice and they don't send the money?

Rick
What the fuck, man!? Let's just make the swap and go!

Gene and Rick both shove the packages across the ice, but both only travel a short distance

Comic sequence of slipping and falling retrieving the packages

Gene
Okay, okay. How about this. Lou will bring you half the drugs. Then you bring half the money.
Then Lou will bring you a quarter of the drugs. Then you bring a quarter of the money.
Then Lou will bring you an eigth of the drugs. Then you bring an eighth of the money ---

Sam
--- That'll take forever!

Gene
Okay, alright. I'll hide the drugs. You hide the money. We each set up a chain of clues leading to the packages ---

Rick pulls out a gun

Rick
--- WAIT, WHAT. NO. WE ARE NOT DOING THIS.

Lou
Look! I'm a speech and debate teacher! I can resolve this tension.
Alright, alright. How about this. You shoot Gene, then Sam, then me, then yourself.

BANG BANG BANG BANG
